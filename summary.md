# 🧠 PSYCHOLOGICAL DRIVERS

## 🔴 PAINS & FRUSTRATIONS

- **Insight title:** Project Completion and Quality Concerns
  - % of users: 21%
  - Quote (REAL): "entregan trabajos incompletos, de cualquier manera, edificaciones con humedades, sin terminar, con afectación de la seguridad"
  - Implication: Businesses should prioritize quality control and timely completion to build trust and avoid negative word-of-mouth.

- **Insight title:** Poor Post-Sale Service
  - % of users: 21%
  - Quote (REAL): "después de haber logrado la venta, y empieza el proceso de entrega, ahí es cuando realmente se muestran como son. entregan trabajos incompletos"
  - Implication: Companies must ensure consistent service quality after the sale to maintain customer satisfaction and loyalty.

- **Insight title:** Lack of Transparency
  - % of users: 17%
  - Quote (REAL): "escritos que parece que estuvieran fabricando aún el papel"
  - Implication: Improving transparency in documentation processes is crucial to addressing user concerns about the legitimacy of transactions.

- **Insight title:** Attitude and Respect Issues
  - % of users: 17%
  - Quote (REAL): "las asesoras, una vez han cogido el dinero de los clientes, empiezan a bufar, y tratarlo a uno como si apestara"
  - Implication: Businesses should focus on training customer service representatives to maintain professionalism and respect throughout the transaction.

- **Insight title:** Unfulfilled Promises
  - % of users: 17%
  - Quote (REAL): "zonas comunes prometidas, que a saber si un día entregarán"
  - Implication: Fulfilled promises are essential for building trust and ensuring customer satisfaction. Businesses must ensure that promised features and services are delivered as agreed.
